Why is the Capybara a trending figure?

  • Capybara's have portrayed tame behaviour around all forms of animals and humans.

Over the past year, people have become aware of this animal. Being one of many I want to showcase this animal as many may not be aware of its existence. The capybara is one the world's most friendliest creature to exist.
